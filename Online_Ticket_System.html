Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a ticket?

Fill out the form in the "Submit a Ticket" section and click "Submit".

How do I check my ticket status?

Enter your Ticket ID in the "Check Ticket Status" section and click "Check Status".

What happens after I submit a ticket?

Our support team will review your issue and respond via the email address provided.
